How can we help you?

What is voluntario?

Voluntario is a free volunteering event management system that can be used by both the event organizers and volunteers. Our project is website based and is tailored for volunteers who wish to help out their community as well as to organizers, who are seeking volunteers for their organizations. This project is a rating-based system in which the organizers and volunteers can rate their experience and provide a short review in order to improve the quality of the events being posted and help future volunteers and organizers. The volunteers will be able to explore the list of ongoing events, filter them to their desired parameters, enlist in events, subscribe to mailing lists to stay updated, and rate the organizer. The organizers will be responsible for posting events, sending announcements, and rating the volunteers.

Do I need qualifications?

Not usually, no. Some volunteering opportunities require certain skills such as language requirement, or may require certain previous experience that are suited towards teh event but organisations often provide training. Often organisations are looking for personal skills, such as being able to get on with a wide variety of people, being reliable and being enthusiastic about a particular interest or cause - rather than academic qualifications.

How old do I have to be to Volunteer?

According to the rules and regulations in UAE, you must be over the age of 16 to partake in any of the volunteering oppurtunities offered. Having said that, some opportunities may have a higher age restrictions. That is typically because of the type of opportunity. You can filter your search for opportunities on our site to return volunteer opportunities for a specific age range.
